# RecoverOps Strategy

## Positioning

RecoverOps is an action system for finance operators. It does not just summarize spend. It discovers recoverable dollars, assembles proof, drafts the claim, and tracks recovery to completion.

## Wedge

Start with ecommerce brands and high-growth startups with recurring vendor spend and fragmented tooling.

The strongest wedge is:

- Shopify-first ecommerce brands
- SaaS, logistics, ads, and contractor spend
- teams feeling margin pressure
- no dedicated spend-audit function

Initial use case:

> We find money you are already owed within the first 7 days.

## Why This Wedge Works

- Pain is concrete and frequent
- ROI is easy to prove
- Data sources are messy but narrow enough for an MVP
- The demo is visually strong: recovered cash beats abstract productivity
- The expansion path is large: recovery agent to spend control to autonomous finance ops

## Ideal Customer Profile

Company:

- 10 to 200 employees
- $1M to $50M revenue
- high monthly vendor spend
- multiple vendors across payments, shipping, ads, SaaS, and back office
- manual invoice or refund review

Buyer:

- founder
- COO
- head of finance
- finance ops lead

Buying trigger:

- margin compression
- cost-cutting mandate
- post-growth finance cleanup
- suspected vendor billing issues

## Product Principles

1. Lead with money found, not AI.
2. Every finding must show evidence.
3. Human approval stays in the loop.
4. Recoveries should be ranked by confidence and value.
5. The workflow should close the loop: identify, claim, track, recover.

## Long-Term Platform

RecoverOps can expand into:

- continuous vendor spend monitoring
- SaaS seat optimization
- refund and rebate management
- contract term enforcement
- procurement intelligence
- autonomous finance operations
