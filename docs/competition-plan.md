# 8-Week Competition Plan

## Week 1: Focus

- Lock ICP: Shopify and DTC brands first
- Define recovery categories
- Build first demo workflow
- Start outreach list of 50 operators

Success metric:

- 10 discovery conversations booked

## Week 2: Data

- Add CSV upload for vendor exports
- Normalize invoices and statements
- Build initial recovery scoring rules

Success metric:

- first real customer data reviewed manually

## Week 3: Detection

- Detect duplicates, missed credits, late refunds, and suspicious fees
- Rank findings by value and confidence
- Improve evidence display

Success metric:

- $25k recoverable identified across pilots

## Week 4: Recovery Packets

- Generate claim packet per finding
- Draft vendor-specific emails
- Track approval and submission status

Success metric:

- 10 claims approved by users

## Week 5: Follow-Up

- Add reminders and claim timeline
- Add response tracking
- Improve suggested next actions

Success metric:

- first recovered dollars confirmed

## Week 6: Pilot Proof

- Run 10 to 20 audits
- Measure precision, dollars found, and dollars recovered
- Collect testimonials and screenshots

Success metric:

- $100k recoverable identified

## Week 7: Polish

- Improve dashboard and demo story
- Add confidence explanations
- Create final pitch narrative

Success metric:

- 3 paying or signed pilot customers

## Week 8: Submission

- Record product demo
- Prepare traction summary
- Show before/after customer evidence
- Submit with clear business model

Target metrics:

- 20 audits
- $100k+ recoverable identified
- $10k+ recovered or in active claim
- 3 paying customers
- sub-10 minute time to first finding
